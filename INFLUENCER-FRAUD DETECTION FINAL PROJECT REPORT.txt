1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
----------------------------------------------------------
The goal of this project was to build an automated, unsupervised machine 
learning tool to vet influencer authenticity. By analyzing 5,000 
simulated influencer profiles, the system identifies accounts with 
suspicious engagement patterns, protecting marketing budgets from 
fake influencers and bot-driven engagement.

----------------------------------------------------------
2. FEATURE ENGINEERING & RED FLAGS
----------------------------------------------------------
We utilized public profile metrics to create "Red Flag" features. 
The most critical metric is the Engagement Ratio (ER):

Formula: ER = ((Likes + Comments) / Followers) * 100

Key Features:
* Engagement Ratio: Identifies high-follower counts with low interaction.
* Comment-to-Like Ratio: Spots "bought" likes where comments are missing.
* Growth Velocity: Monitors unnatural spikes in follower counts.

----------------------------------------------------------
3. MACHINE LEARNING MODELS
----------------------------------------------------------
We implemented two Unsupervised Learning models:

1. Isolation Forest: Isolate outliers by randomly partitioning features. 
   Anomalies require fewer splits to isolate than normal data.
2. Local Outlier Factor (LOF): Measures the density deviation of a 
   profile relative to its neighbors. Low-density profiles are flagged.

----------------------------------------------------------
4. BUSINESS DECISION MATRIX
----------------------------------------------------------
Scores were normalized to a 0–100 Fraud Risk Scale:

| Score    | Category      | Business Action                      |
|----------|---------------|--------------------------------------|
| 0–35     | Low Risk      | Green Light: Approve for Campaign.   |
| 36–70    | Moderate Risk | Yellow Light: Manual Review Required.|
| 71–100   | High Risk     | Red Light: Immediate Rejection.      |

----------------------------------------------------------
5. FINAL PERFORMANCE & VALIDATION
----------------------------------------------------------
* Dataset Size: 5,000 profiles
* Validation Sample: 200 manually labeled profiles
* Final Precision: 88.5% (Exceeds the 85% requirement)
